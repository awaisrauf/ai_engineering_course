Final Project

The final project is the centrepiece of this module (45% of grade). You will build a complete AI system demonstrating your ability to design, implement, and evaluate production-ready AI applications.

Timeline

Milestone Due Date Weight

Requirements

Projects may be completed individually or in pairs. Your project must demonstrate competency in at least three of:

  • Prompt engineering and in-context learning
  • Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
  • Fine-tuning (LoRA, instruction tuning, RLHF)
  • Multimodal AI (vision, audio)
  • Agentic systems (tool use, planning, multi-agent)
  • Production deployment (APIs, optimisation, monitoring)
