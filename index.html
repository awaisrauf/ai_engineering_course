What is this course about

AI Engineering teaches you how to build production-ready AI systems using modern foundation models. You will learn to work with Large Language Models (LLMs), vision models, and multimodal systems. The course covers prompt engineering, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), fine-tuning techniques, building AI agents, and deploying scalable AI services. Rather than focusing solely on model architecture, this course emphasises the practical skills needed to deploy AI in real-world applications. By the end, you'll be equipped to build and maintain AI systems that solve genuine problems.
